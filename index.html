关于我

My name is Teresa Huang, graduate student of psychology, now focus on user experience design and data visualization.

I'm a psychology scientific researcher by training. I hold a Bachelor degree in psychology and then a Master degree in cognitive neuroscience, I did researches (about: auditory learning ; speech perception) in Beijing Normal University - State Key Laboratory of Cognitive Neuroscience and Learning adopt behavioral and electrophysiological techniques. More information about me could check my CV/Resume(PDF).
